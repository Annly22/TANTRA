Tote Tales is a creative, hands-on tote bag painting workshop that encourages artistic expression and thoughtful design. Participants will explore colors, patterns, and personal storytelling while painting their own reusable tote bags.The session promotes creativity, individuality, and sustainable practices by transforming a simple tote bag into a functional piece of art that participants can take home and use.
