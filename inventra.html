Rules & Guidelines

  • Open to undergraduate engineering students from recognized institutions.
  • Each team must consist of 2 or 3 members.
  • A valid college ID card is mandatory.
  • To register for the competition, submit your project abstract along with a ₹100 fee; if selected after the filtering process, a ₹200 payment is required to present your paper either online or offline.
  • Strict adherence to time limits is mandatory.
  • All bids placed during the auction are final and non-reversible.
  • Judges' and coordinators' decisions are final.
  • Originality & Research: Judges will assess the novelty of your idea and the depth of your research.
  • Technical Methodology: The logical flow, experimental design, and accuracy of your results must be clearly demonstrated.
  • Presentation & Delivery: You will be graded on the clarity of your speech, slide design, and effective time management.
  • Participants must maintain professional behavior.
  • Any misconduct or equipment damage will lead to disqualification.
  • Evaluation is done based on the above criteria.
  • Winners are selected based on overall performance.
  • The final decision rests with the judging panel.
