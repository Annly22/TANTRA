GRIDCRAFT is a hands-on technical competition where participants design a simple Smart Energy Management Board for a home load. Teams will monitor energy consumption, control electrical loads, and implement logical decision-making in real time. The event tests practical skills in electronics, automation, and smart power systems through real-world energy scenarios.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Students from all departments are eligible.
  • Students from all academic years are eligible.
  • Each team must consist of exactly 4 members.
  • A valid college ID card is mandatory.
  • Round 1: Sensing Module (Measure)
  • Round 2: Switching Module (Control)
  • Round 3: Logic Module (Automate)
  • Round 1: Build a current/voltage sensing module using the provided components.
  • A functional sensing block ready for integration should be prepared by the end of this round.
  • Round 2: Design a relay/SSR-based load switching circuit.
  • A working load control module is the expected output of this round.
  • Round 3: Demonstrate the complete Smart Energy Management Board.
  • Participants must maintain professional behavior.
  • Any misconduct or equipment damage will lead to disqualification.
  • Judges and coordinators decisions are final.
  • Winners are selected based on overall performance.
  • The final decision rests with the judging panel.
