TECHTRIAD is a three-round technical competition designed for Electronics & Communication Engineering students. The event evaluates participants on conceptual clarity, simulation skills, and practical troubleshooting ability. The competition is common for all years and progresses from theory to hands-on problem solving.

Rules & Guidelines

  • Open to Electronics & Communication Engineering (ECE) students.
  • Students from all academic years are eligible.
  • Each team must consist of exactly 2 members.
  • Valid college ID card is mandatory.
  • The competition consists of three rounds conducted sequentially.
  • Teams must qualify each round to advance.
  • Strict adherence to time limits is mandatory.
  • Unfair means or misconduct will result in disqualification.
  • Round 1 : Solve: 20-minute ECE quiz testing conceptual knowledge.
  • Round 2 : Simulate: 30-minute LTspice circuit design and simulation.
  • Round 3 : Survive: 30-minute circuit debugging with bonus quiz.
  • Participants must maintain professional behavior.
  • Any misconduct or equipment damage will lead to disqualification.
  • Instructions from coordinators must be followed strictly.
  • Evaluation is done round wise.
  • Winners are selected based on overall performance.
  • Bonus quiz marks act as tie-breakers.
  • Final decision rests with the judging panel
